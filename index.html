About ResumePro

Founded in 2022 by a team of Silicon Valley recruiters and AI experts, ResumePro emerged from a simple observation: traditional resume building wasn't keeping pace with modern hiring practices. Our founders spent years reviewing thousands of resumes and noticed that even qualified candidates were being filtered out by ATS systems or failing to effectively communicate their value to employers.

Our mission is to democratize access to career opportunities by providing job seekers with the same tools and insights used by top professionals. We believe everyone deserves a chance to showcase their true potential, and we're committed to using cutting-edge AI technology to help people create resumes that open doors to their dream careers.
